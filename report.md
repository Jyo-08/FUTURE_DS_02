# Customer Retention Intelligence

## Project Overview

Customer Retention Intelligence is a Business Intelligence dashboard that transforms raw customer data into meaningful business insights. The application enables organizations to monitor customer retention, identify churn patterns, evaluate financial impact, and support strategic decision-making through an interactive analytics dashboard.

The project combines a Python analytics engine with a React frontend to create an end-to-end data analytics solution suitable for business stakeholders and decision-makers.

---

# Problem Statement

Organizations often possess large volumes of customer data but lack intuitive tools to transform that information into actionable business intelligence.

Without clear visualization and analytics:

* Customer churn patterns remain hidden.
* Revenue risks are difficult to estimate.
* High-risk customer segments are not easily identified.
* Business decisions become reactive instead of proactive.

Customer Retention Intelligence addresses these challenges by converting customer datasets into executive dashboards and analytical reports.

# Core Features

## Executive Overview

Displays high-level business metrics including:

* Total Customers
* Retained Customers
* Churned Customers
* Churn Rate
* Average Customer Tenure
* Average Monthly Charges

---

## Customer Health Overview

Provides an overall snapshot of customer retention through visual indicators and executive metrics.

---

## Customer Behaviour Analytics

Analyzes customer behaviour across:

* Contract Types
* Internet Services
* Payment Methods
* Customer Tenure

This helps identify which customer groups are more likely to churn.

## Revenue Intelligence

Financial analysis including:

* Average Monthly Charges
* Average Total Charges
* Estimated Monthly Revenue
* Revenue at Risk

---

## Business Intelligence

Automatically generated:

* Executive Summary
* Business Recommendations
* Customer Insights

based on the processed analytics.

Displays a preview of the cleaned customer dataset used by the analytics engine.
